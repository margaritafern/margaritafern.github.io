about

Native-born, I live, learn and love in New York City. At the very young age of 12, my natural design creativity influenced me to seek a path where I can bring my ideas to life. Since then, I have become a seasoned designer and front end developer with over 8 years of experience. I have traditional marketing and advertising experience, yet my focus lies in digital design where creating interactive consumer experiences drive me. As of now, I'm continously seeking to be inspired, to envision the unlikely, to work hard for things that are worth it, and most of all, to be surrounded by those who bring out the best in me.
