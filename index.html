about

Native-born, I live, learn and love in New York City. At a very young age, my natural design creativity influenced me to seek a path where I can bring my ideas to life. Since then, I have become a seasoned digital designer and front end web developer. I also have a strong background in information technology along with traditional marketing and advertising experience, although, my focus lies in digital design where creating interactive consumer experiences drive me. As of now, I'm continuously seeking to be inspired, to envision the unlikely, to work hard for things that are worth it, and most of all, to be surrounded by those who bring out the best in me.
