about

Native-born, I live, learn and love in New York City. At a very young age, my natural design creativity influenced me to seek a path where I can bring my ideas to life. Since then, I have expanded my knowledge and skills within diverse areas. I have studied multiple areas including: digital design, front end web development, information technology, business management, advertising and marketing. I am currently finishing my B.F.A at the Fashion Institute of Technology for Advertising Design and minoring in Business Management. As of now, I'm continuously seeking to be inspired, to envision the unlikely, to work hard for things that are worth it and most of all, to be surrounded by those who bring out the best in me.
