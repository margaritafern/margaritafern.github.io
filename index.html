about

Native-born, I live, learn and love in New York City. At a very young age, my natural creative and analytical persona influenced me to seek a technical path where I can bring my ideas to life. Since then, I have studied and expanded my technical skills within diverse areas including, but not limited to: Information Technology, Responsive Digital Design, Web Development and Python Programming. I am currently continuing my advanced studies in Information Technology while pursuing a career in Cyber Security.

Within my professional journey, I am continuously seeking to be inspired, to envision the unlikely, to work hard for things that are worth it and most of all, to be surrounded by those who bring out the best in me.
