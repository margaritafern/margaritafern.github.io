about

Welcome! While native born in New York City, Margarita is currently living, learning and in love with Tampa, Florida. At a very young age, her natural design creativity influenced her to seek a path where she can bring her ideas to life. Margarita is a Technology graduate of NPower, with Professional Development mentorship at Facebook, Microsoft and Ernst & Young in New York City. In addition, she has a strong background in Customer Service, along with traditional marketing and digital designing experience. Although, her focus lies in digital design where creating interactive consumer experiences drives her. As of now, she is continously seeking to be inspired, to envision the unlikely, to work hard for things that are worth it while always being surrounded by those who bring out the best in her.
